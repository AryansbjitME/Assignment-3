Welcome to our Plant Nursery

Discover a wide variety of beautiful plants for your garden.

Rose

The Rose is a classic and elegant flower known for its beauty and fragrance.

Lavender

Lavender is a versatile herb with aromatic flowers that are used in various applications.

Succulents

Succulents are low-maintenance plants that come in a variety of unique shapes and sizes.

Lotus

The lotus flower has a variety of different meanings in different cultures.

Lily

The true lilies are erect perennial plants with leafy stems, scaly bulbs, usually narrow leaves, and solitary or clustered flowers.

Marigold

Marigold flower is born in winter. Scientific name of marigold flower is 'tagetes'.

Hibiscus

Hibiscus is a genus of several species of shrubs, herbs and trees belonging to the Malvaceae family.

Sunflower

The 'flower' of the common sunflower is actually a pseudanthium, or flowerhead, made up of many small flowers.

Bougainvillea

A favorite in tropical locales, this colorful climber can also be grown annually if you don't happen to live in a sunny spot.
